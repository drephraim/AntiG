"I don't think we should be here," Elara whispered, her flashlight beam trembling against the damp stone walls. The air smelled of old dust and secrets.
Kael ignored her, running his hand along the intricate carvings. "The map said the codex is here. And where the codex is, the Key of Shadows rests."
"The Key is a myth, Kael!"
"Is it?" He pushed against a loose stone. With a grinding sound, a hidden panel slid open, revealing a dusty pedestal. Upon it rested a glowing, crimson gem.
"The Key," Kael breathed, stepping forward.
Elara grabbed his arm. "Wait! It could be trapped."
But it was too late. As his fingers brushed the gem, the crypt doors slammed shut behind them, plunging them into darkness.
