The voice had faded, leaving only the oppressive silence and the crimson glow. Kael stared at the gem, the Key of Shadows. He felt a strange pull toward it, a whisper in his blood.
Elara was examining the walls, running her hands over the carvings. "These runes... they're not just decorative. Kael, come look at this."
He tore his gaseous gaze from the gem and joined her. 
"Look here," she pointed to a series of deep etchings. "It tells a story. Three trials. The first is the Trial of Flame."
As if responding to her words, the stone floor began to warm. The temperature in the crypt rose steadily. 
"We need to find the answer," Kael said, urgency edging into his voice. "Before we roast alive."
He rushed back to the pedestal. The gem. The Key. It must hold the answer to the first trial.
